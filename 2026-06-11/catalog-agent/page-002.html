★ Insight ───────────────────────────────────── The dependency graph tells you your service's role. The existing claims-payment-system flows downward: settlement calculates → disbursement executes → supplier/subrogation handle specifics. But there's no feedback loop — nothing currently confirms that payments actually landed. Your service closes that loop by consuming bank confirmation files and feeding results back upstream to the reserve calculator and status tracker.

Watch the subrogation edge case. Recovery payments are money flowing into the claims system from third parties — the opposite direction from settlements. The existing payment-reconciliation-service in billing-payments handles inbound premium flows, but recovery payments are not premiums. They're claim recoveries. Your service needs to reconcile both directions: outbound settlements and inbound recoveries. That's a scope distinction worth clarifying with claims-engineering early.
